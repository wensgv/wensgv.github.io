All Authors: M. Pichumani, M. Giuliani, W. Gonz\'alez-Vi\~nas


Title: 1-D Cluster Array at the Three Phase Contact Line in Diluted
Colloids Subjected to A.C. Electric Fields


Description: 

Experimental video clips (front view, five times faster than the real time)
for the forcing conditions 1.8 Vpp, 0.8 Hz (Clip A) and 2.2 Vpp, 3.0 Hz
(Clip B). The field of view (length) for both clips is of 0.6 mm.
- Clip A corresponds to the Figure 2 (A to G) shown in the Letter. The
various stages are explained in the text. 
- Clip B refers to the high applied frequency case where the clusters do
not move significantly (see text). The intrinsic brightness variation
inside the clusters can be seen (frame t_0575.809.jpg till the end).


Total No. of Files: 3


File names: ClipA.mpg, ClipB.mpg and README.TXT


File types: .mpg (2 files) and .txt (1 file)
